Decatur Business Center

Decatur Business Center

NAIOP Spotlight Award recipient for best flex building in Southern Nevada. For Lease. An 87,000 square foot concrete tilt-up building offering office / showroom / warehouse space. Currently ready for Tenant Improvements. Located in the heart of the desirable southwest submarket. Decatur Business Center is strategically located minutes from the I-15, I-215, McCarran Airport and the Las Vegas Strip.

Fully insulated, high performance glass, skylights in the warehouse area, 32’ minimum clear height warehouse. Fully fire sprinklered, ESFR capable, 12’ X 14’ overhead doors, 3.2 parking spaces per 1000 SF, natural gas available. Common loading dock. Second floor office / showroom served by an upscale lobby, elevator, and internal corridors. Hi-Tech Surveillance system with keycard access. Corporate identity allowed on the building. Electronic / digital marquee signage on Decatur available. Can design and build custom tenant improvements to client’s specifications.
